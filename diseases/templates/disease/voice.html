In PD, the muscles used for speech may become weaker, leading to a softer, more monotone voice, a slower rate of speech, and difficulty with articulation. Voice recognition technology can help by accurately transcribing speech, making it easier for people with PD to communicate their thoughts and ideas.

There are also specialized voice recognition software programs designed specifically for people with PD. These programs use algorithms to analyze the user's voice and provide feedback on how to improve their speech.
